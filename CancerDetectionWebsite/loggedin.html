BU ENG ECE 601 Project

Cancer Detection Team

Kaggle Project

The goal of this project is Personalized Medicine: Redefining Cancer Treatment. We are predict the effect of Genetic Variants to enable Personalized Medicine. Using text mining and machine learing, we are classifying research papers about genetic mutations. There are 9 classes.
1. Likely Loss-of-function
2. Likely Gain-of-function
3. Neutral
4. Loss-of-function
5. Likely Neutral
6. Inconclusive
7. Gain-of-function
8. Likely Switch-of-function
9. Switch-of-function
